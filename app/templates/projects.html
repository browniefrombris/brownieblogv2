Repairing Accura Bouncemaster Bulb Flash


Antique flash bulb for film camera, believed to have belonged to my Dad's grandfather. Internal battery and capacitor compartment damaged by corrosion from leaking battery. Simple electronics, should be an easy fix!

eBay listing for identical item

Notes:
Battery-Capacitor (B-C) System
Battery = 22.5V
Capacitor = 100 microfarad 22.5WV
Looks very similar to the Nikon BC-5. Manual available here.

Battery ruined (also wrong battery - was a 1.5V AA). Capacitor looks in good shape externally.
Corrosive damage to component connecting battery and capacitor makes it difficult to identify but looks like a resistor.
Confirmed on image in eBay listing above showing resistor with: ?BROWN | GREEN | RED (?1500ohms, tolerance band not visible).
New 1500ohm capacitor soldered in place.

Unicell 505 22.5V battery purchased online from batteryspecialists.com, awaiting delivery.
